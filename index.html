About me

Nicolas is a lifelong learner (use the arrow to select your preffered choice.). He's born and raised in France in the suburb of Paris. He discovered two years after highschool graduation that he loves maths and sciences, and got accepted late at Jussieu. He prepared during three years for the admission competition of the Ecoles Normales Supérieures, which is impossible to get in unless you prepare in one of the mega top elite parisian classes préparatoires from where Nobels and Fields medal come from, and he absolute failed twice. He slowed down maths to avoid stoping loving it, and started to learn Russian at the INALCO. Because of that, he needed an extra year for his Maitrise, but he doesn't regret it (although it doesn't make it easier to explain his background in job interviews...). He then fell in love with language learning. He is now studying Estonian and Swedish more than the others. He is autistic, you should now. If you meet him and think he is strange, it's good! It means he considers he doesn't have to pretend to be normal around you, and it's a big releif for him because faking normality makes people more comfortable, but it's so much energy that he prefers to spend otherwise. He doesn't want to be a hero, he wants to be useful, happy, and extremely rich. He has a lot to say, and hopes you will enjoy visiting his mind through this site.
