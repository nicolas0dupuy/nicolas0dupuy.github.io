Philosophy & Operation

I run an independent mathematical backend service designed to link theoretical research and industrial applications. As an autistic individual, my neurocognition dictates an optimal operational model distinct from traditional environments. I optimize for deep focus, prioritizing high-value output over administrative presence.

Structured for frictionless deployment, collaborations are executed asynchronously and governed by a certified Employer of Record, allowing clients to interface directly with an autonomous, white-label mathematical backend without incurring legal or administrative overhead.

A few words regarding the fundamental motivations behind this practice, from the perspective of my own objectives. I want to deploy two decades of multidisciplinary study where my skills are a scarce resource, while preserving my freedom for continuous learning. In particular, after seven years of lecturing at the Paris School of Business, I encountered economics and finance for the first time, and their mechanics fascinated me enough to start exploring their underlying mathematics. Much of this domain remains new to me; my financial knowledge has just begun maturing, having bought my first stock years after some of my own Data Management students had already done so. However, driven by a desire to explore and contribute, my atypical position called for an atypical solution, leading me to self-direct a Shadow PhD in Quantitative Finance. I intend to learn by strengthening academic and institutional teams, applying my established experience in computational physics, mathematics, data science, and machine learning to their challenges. I want to forge intellectual ties with experts and technical professionals who recognize the intrinsic value of my autonomous mathematical backend, and guarantee the sustained generation of high-value output by bypassing the systemic frictions of my autism while fully exploiting its cognitive advantages.

I have always been fundamentally cross-disciplinary. I did not land in a laboratory blending material science, mineralogy, and cosmochemistry by accident. Before that, I explored multiple concurrent paths, spanning fundamental mathematics, physics, and Russian language and literature. I once tried to domesticate my mind, only to realize that such constraint merely bridled it. Permitting a continuous intellectual rotation—one that naturally uncovers structural connections between disparate fields—proved to be the optimal use of this cognitive architecture.

I invite you to examine my service architecture below. It is organized by tier, progressing from foundational data structuring to advanced optimization and generative modeling. This hierarchy is a deliberate operational mechanism, allowing me to allocate my cognitive bandwidth systematically, aligning my project planning with the energy constraints of my autistic neurology—a subject I will expand upon in my technical logs to provide complete operational transparency. Furthermore, to maintain this transparency, each tier includes a rigorous self-evaluation of my current technical proficiency. I welcome the opportunity to examine your computational challenges. Thank you for reading.
